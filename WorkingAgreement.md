#Culture

##Trust
- We are open and will communicate honestly.
- We will ask for help early without fear of judgement.
- We will admit mistakes quickly and focus on solutions instead of blame.
- Assume team members have good intentions.

##Conflict
- Keep arguments and disagreements pointed at the issue.
- We will not criticize personal character.

##Commitment
- Showing up to meeting on time and being present.
- Actually doing the work.
- Maintining personal time commitments to get the work done.
- Make sure expectations are clear before starting work.
- Make sure everyone agrees to the project and is on the same page.

##Accountability
- Give feedback in a constructive way.
- Define clear expectations for team members.
- Offering support as a team.
- Offer immediate feedback.

##Results
- Create a public declaration of results.
- Prioritize collective outcomes over individual ones.
- Get a satisfactory result.
- Be flexable to get the wanted results.

#Meetings
- We will meet at least one time outside of classtime.
- Meetings will be either in person or in Discord.
- A Different person will take notes each meeting.
- If someone can't make a meeting, communicate with the team before hand and review the notes for the meeting.
- Meeting notes will be stored on Discord.
- Have a checklist to follow up on work items.
- If someone misses a meeting or is late, review the notes from the meeting and follow up with the group.

#Communications
- Commucation platform is Discord.
- GitHub for issues, tasks, and documentation.
- Email for anything formal.
- Text message or phone call for anything urgent.
- Response time for something urgent will be 24 hours.
- Response time for non urgent items will be 72 hours.

#Decisions
- Gather all relavent information to make decisions.
- If a consensus can't be reached, we take a majority vote.
- Identify the problem and come up with solutions as a team.
- Once a decision is made, we all support it.

#Development
- Work will be broken into GitHub issues.
- Each issue will have:
	- A clear description.
	- A deadline.
	- An assigned person.
- Work will be distributed evenly for each team member and decided at meetings.
- Work will be completed on our own laptops and pushed to GitHub.
- It is each team members responsability to complete work on time. If work is consistently not completed on the person will be held accountable during meetings.

#Workflow
- Get the assignment, review it, discuss it with the team, create a checklist on the task, complete the task, and review the task.
- Issues will be created during the meeting.
- Anyone can create an issue.
- Issues will be moved once they are reviewed.
- The person who reviews the task will move the task.
- Moving issues will be done as a team during meetings.

#Agreement Review
We will review this:
- After our first milestone.
- Mid-project.
- Before final submission.
